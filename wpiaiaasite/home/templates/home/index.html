American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics

Worcester Polytechnic Institute Student Chapter

We're the WPI Student Chapter of the AIAA, a national aerospace professional society. On campus, we're one of the largest special interest clubs and we host a variety of events from guest speakers to design challenges. AIAA also has several subcommittees that work with hands on aerospace projects invovling RC jet engines, drones, and model rockets. Any WPI student, regardless of major or experience level, is eligible to join our student chapter.
