The Team

HPRC is a student organization at WPI and a subcommittee of the AIAA. The team is lead by the Captain and the Officer Board, composed of eleven dedicated members. It is organized into three divisions, Rocket, Payload, and Electronics & Programming, each run by a division lead. Additionally, the team's advisor is Professor Blandino.
