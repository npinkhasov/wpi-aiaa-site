The Team

HPRC is an extracurricular student organization at WPI. The team is lead by the captain, the project engineering leads, and the general officer board. The current members fulfiling these roles are shown below, contact them for more information on their respective positions. Competition work is organized into three divisions: Rocket, Payload, and Electronics & Programming, each run by a division lead. The team's faculty advisor is Professor Blandino.
